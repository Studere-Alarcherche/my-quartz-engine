# Six Units

These units are entry routes, not a closed syllabus.

1. Text, Time, and Signs
2. Music, Ineffability, and Form
3. Visuality, Attention, and Image
4. Space, Sensory History, and Social Time
5. Writing and Course-Making
6. Evidence, Packets, and Public Reading Paths

Each unit should return to Proust. Secondary sources serve the reading path only when they sharpen a Proustian scene or question.
