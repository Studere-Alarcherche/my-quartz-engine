# Course Thesis

Proustian 2.0 reads Proust as an apprenticeship in signs: a long training in how perception, memory, desire, error, and art teach a reader to recognize truth indirectly.

The course does not treat Proust as a topic to summarize. It treats the novel as a field of encounters where reading becomes a discipline of attention.

## Working Thesis

The reader enters Proust through scenes where something demands interpretation before it can be understood: a taste, a phrase of music, a painted detail, a social gesture, a room, a name, or a form of self-deception.
