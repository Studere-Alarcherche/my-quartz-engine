# Question Gradient

The question gradient keeps public reading pages focused. A question should move from scene to method, then back to Proust.

## Starting Questions

- What pressure does this scene place on the reader?
- What sign appears before it is understood?
- What kind of mistake, delay, or misrecognition organizes the scene?
- Which secondary lens clarifies the scene without replacing Proust?
- What form of attention does the passage train?

## Direction

Good public questions should remain small enough to verify and strong enough to open a reading path.
